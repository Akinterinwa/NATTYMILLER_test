Who is Natty?

Natty Miller is a Pan Afrikan/ Int'l performer, music composer and recording artist of Yoruba descent. He was born and raised in Port-harcourt, Nigeria and discovered music at a very early age. Christened "Ogo Boma Seyi" and later on renamed "Henry Jnr II".
